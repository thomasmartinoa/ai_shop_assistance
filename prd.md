Product Name

Shopkeeper AI-Assistant (Voice-First Retail Copilot)

1. Product Overview
1.1 Problem Statement

Small retail shopkeepers in Kerala and similar regions rely heavily on manual processes for:

Inventory tracking

Billing and payment collection

Locating items in shops

Profit and loss calculations

These processes are:

Error-prone

Time-consuming

Difficult to scale

Not friendly to non-technical users

Most existing POS systems are English-first, UI-heavy, and costly, making them unsuitable for small shopkeepers.

1.2 Solution Summary

Shopkeeper AI-Assistant is a Malayalam voice-driven AI system that allows shopkeepers to manage daily operations using natural speech.

The assistant enables:

Voice-based stock checking

Voice-driven billing

Item location lookup (shelf/rack)

GPay / UPI QR payment generation

Automated profit & loss reports

All interactions happen through Malayalam voice commands, minimizing learning curve and maximizing adoption.
